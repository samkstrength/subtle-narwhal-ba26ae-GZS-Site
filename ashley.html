About Ashley

Ashley Montgomery

Ashley Montgomery is a strength coach at Ground Zero Strength specializing in women's strength training. She works with women navigating the unique challenges of female physiology — from new lifters intimidated by traditional gym environments to women training through pregnancy and rebuilding postpartum.


Her clients are motivated, capable women who haven't been given clear, practical guidance on how strength training should actually work for them. Ashley provides that guidance — with structured programming, real coaching, and a long-term approach that fits their lives.
