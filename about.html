Five years in, that idea hasn't changed. The Pacific Northwest's only Starting Strength affiliate gym.

The Coaches

Who you'll work with.

Sam Photo
Sam Krapf
Head Coach, SSC — Owner

Sam is a Starting Strength Coach and the owner of Ground Zero Strength. A former collegiate baseball coach and athlete, he went full-time into strength coaching in 2021 and opened GZS to bring serious barbell coaching to Twin Falls.

He holds a Bachelor's in Biology and a Master's in Sports Performance. He coaches lifters from complete beginners to competitive powerlifters, specializing in scaling the barbell lifts for any age, ability, or limitation. Outside the gym he competes in jiu-jitsu, hunts elk and mule deer in the Idaho backcountry, and trains through his own Scheuermann's kyphosis — which informs his approach to every client with structural limitations.

Nicole Sharp
Physical Therapist — Treat & Train

Nicole is a licensed physical therapist and strength coach specializing in the Treat & Train model — combining PT assessment and treatment with barbell-based strength programming for clients with limitations, injuries, and movement issues.

Her approach is built for the people the standard model ignores: those who've been told not to lift, who've been working around problems for years, or who need someone who understands both rehab and the barbell.

The Philosophy

Systematic. Progressive. Honest.

GZS is built on the Starting Strength method — the most effective and scalable barbell training system developed. But it's not dogma. Sam uses it as a foundation and adapts it to the individual.


The goal is always the same: get you stronger, keep you healthy, and build a training practice you can sustain for decades. Not a 12-week program. Not a transformation challenge. A practice you keep forever.
