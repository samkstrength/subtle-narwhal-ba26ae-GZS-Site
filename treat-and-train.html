About Nicole

Nicole Sharp, PT

Nicole Sharp is a licensed physical therapist and strength coach based at Ground Zero Strength in Twin Falls. Her approach combines clinical PT assessment with barbell-based strength programming — treating the limitation and building strength on top of it simultaneously.


She works with clients who have movement issues, injury histories, or structural limitations that require more than a generic program. She brings both the clinical eye and the coaching skill to get you stronger, safely.
